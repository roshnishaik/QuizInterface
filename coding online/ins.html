PRECEPTS

Read the instructions carefully before you proceed to take test

1.A team shall consist at max. of 2 members.
2.The online round comprises of 30 questions to be answered in 30 minutes.(NO Negative Marking)
3.In our user friendly interface(Our interface is best viewed in Chrome Browser),
 we have entitled you with a timer thereby providing the duration of time still left.
4.Teams answering maximum number of answers correctly shall be called for ROUND 2
5.During the test Status of Questions(Answered/Un Answered) can be obtained by clicking
 on submitted answers button.
6.Questions can be viewed randomly by clicking Corresponding questions in Question Catalogue table
 and you can modify your answer too.
7. you may press the end test button, if the test is completed before the timer ends.
